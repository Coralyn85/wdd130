Maui, Hawaii

Hawaii is the 49th state to become a part of the United States of America. It was admitted as a state the same year as Alaska, just a few months before. Prior to becoming part of the USA, it was called the Kingdom of Hawaii. Lahaina was once the capital of the state, but it was moved to Oahu as time went on. Hawaii is a popular vacation destination and its economy relies on the tourist industry. Recently Maui Hawaii, specifically the former state capital of Lahaina, experienced the worst natural disaster in the past 100 years in the USA with a devastating wildfire that killed 102 people.
